Our Rooms

A Unique Place of Authentic Greek Hospitality

The Erato Apartments offer fully equipped and traditionally decorated rooms that will cover any of your needs and will provide you the relaxation you seek during your vacation. Whether you are travelling as a couple, with friends or family, you will definitely find the ideal accommodation according to your needs.Each our rooms is equipped with the kitchenette where you can prepare a light snack, wardrobe or closet, clothes racks, linens, soap and shampoo. Furnished balconies give you the opportunity to enjoy the view and your coffee without even living your room. But in case you want to have a breakfast, relax, or arrange a business meeting, you may do this at our café-bar.For the foreign visitors the airport or train transfer is possible for the extra charge by prior arrangement.
